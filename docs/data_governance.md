# Data Governance

## What We Store
- title
- summary
- url
- byline
- source
- publishedAt
- language
- canonicalUrl
- text (cleaned)

## Retention
- Raw HTML: 30 days
- Cleaned text: 6 months
- Summaries: 6 months

## Deletion
- Hard delete within 7 days of request

## Constraints
- Respect robots.txt
- No ingestion of paywalled content
